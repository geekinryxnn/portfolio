About Me

I'm a street photographer passionate about capturing the fleeting moments and vibrant energy of city life. I focus on candid shots that tell stories and reveal the authentic spirit of urban environments. My work aims to connect with viewers on an emotional level, inviting them to see the beauty in everyday moments.

I'm especially interested in capturing the unique perspectives of teens within the urban landscape. I believe their energy and style add a dynamic layer to street photography.
